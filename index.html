About

Furniture consists of movable objects like chairs, tables, and beds, which are used to support human activities such as seating, eating, sleeping, and storage. It can be functional, decorative, or both, and is made from a wide variety of materials including wood, metal, plastic, and fabric. Common types include seating furniture, sleeping furniture, storage furniture, and dining furniture.
